\subsection{Detailed Description}
State machines can be used to express the behavior of part of a system. Behavior is modeled as a traversal of a graph of state nodes interconnected by one or more joined transition arcs that are triggered by the dispatching of series of (event) occurrences. During this traversal, the state machine executes a series of activities associated with various elements of the state machine. \mbox{[}\href{http://www.omg.org/spec/UML/}{\tt U\-M\-L Superstructure 2.\-4.\-1}\mbox{]}
